Project Overview
This project involves the comprehensive analysis of a Global Superstore e-commerce dataset. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive Power BI dashboard to identify best-selling products, regional performance, and historical sales trends to support data-driven business decisions.

Key Features & Insights

Sales Trend Analysis: Leveraged time-series visualizations to identify peak sales periods and seasonal fluctuations from 2011 to 2014.


Product Performance: Ranked products by sales volume and category to pinpoint high-revenue drivers like Technology and Office Supplies.


Geographic Intelligence: Utilized regional treemaps to identify California and New York as top-performing markets.


Dynamic KPI Tracking: Real-time monitoring of Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity through interactive KPI cards.

Technical Skills Applied

Data Cleaning: Performed extensive data transformation and cleaning using Power Query to handle date formatting and numerical accuracy.


DAX Modeling: Developed custom measures (Total Sales, Total Profit) to calculate business metrics accurately.


Business Storytelling: Applied design principles to create an intuitive, user-friendly layout that tells a story of business growth and operational bottlenecks.

Tools Used

Power BI Desktop: Dashboard creation and data visualization.


Power Query: Data extraction and transformation.


Kaggle Dataset: Global Superstore E-commerce data.
